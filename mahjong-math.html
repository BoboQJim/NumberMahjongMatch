How to Play Number Mahjong

Number Mahjong is a brain-training math puzzle game that combines classic mahjong solitaire tile-stacking mechanics with arithmetic calculation. Each level presents a target number on a stack of numbered mahjong tiles. Your goal: select exactly 3 unlocked number tiles and use addition (+), subtraction (−), multiplication (×), or division (÷) to calculate the target number.

Core Features

  • 100 progressively challenging levels with unique target numbers and tile layouts
  • Stacked tile mechanics — clear upper tiles to unlock layers beneath
  • Hint system to highlight a valid 3-tile combination when stuck
  • Auto-match for instant automatic clearing of a valid trio
  • Undo moves to reverse mistakes
  • Free to play — no download, no registration
  • Fully responsive on desktop, tablet, and mobile

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Step 1: Look at the target number displayed at the top of the game screen.
  • Step 2: Tap 3 unlocked tiles (tiles not covered by upper layers). Selected tiles appear in the slots below.
  • Step 3: Use + − × ÷ to combine your 3 numbers and reach the target.
  • Step 4: On success, tiles are removed and the layers beneath unlock.
  • Step 5: Clear all tiles on the board to complete the level and unlock the next one.

Pro Tips & Strategies

  • Prioritize top-layer tiles — they unlock the most tiles underneath.
  • Look for simple combos first: e.g., two numbers that multiply close to the target, then add or subtract the third.
  • Use the Hint button when you get stuck — it highlights a valid combination.
  • Practice mental math to improve speed and score fewer moves.
  • Plan several moves ahead — see which tiles will be revealed after each clear.
